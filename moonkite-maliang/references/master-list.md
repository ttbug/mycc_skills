# Moonkite-Maliang Master List  
**World-Class Masters for Each Visual Engine (UMPF Framework)**

---

## A. Photography Engine（摄影引擎）

- **Roger Deakins**：Clean, naturalistic lighting, silhouettes, precise composition. *Blade Runner 2049*, *1917*.
- **Hoyte van Hoytema**：Large format, shallow depth of field, IMAX aesthetic, grounded sci-fi. *Interstellar*, *Tenet*.
- **Emmanuel Lubezki**：Natural light, wide-angle lenses, long takes, fluid movement. *The Revenant*, *Birdman*.
- **Fan Ho**：High-contrast black and white, dramatic lighting, shafts of light, Hong Kong street life, geometric composition.
- **Daido Moriyama**：High contrast, grainy black and white, blur, snapshot aesthetic, gritty street photography.
- **Liam Wong**：Cyberpunk aesthetic, neon colors, nocturnal cityscapes, rain, cinematic atmosphere.
- **Steve McCurry**：Vivid colors, cultural depth, piercing portraits, strong narrative storytelling.
- **Christopher Doyle**：Dreamy, saturated colors, motion blur, neon lights, Wong Kar-wai–style romantic chaos.
- **Saul Leiter**：Abstract street photography, reflections, condensation, muted palette, layered compositions.
- **Gregory Crewdson**：Cinematic tableau, staged lighting, suburban surrealism, eerie stillness.
- **Henri Cartier-Bresson**：The decisive moment, candid street photography, geometric balance, black and white.
- **William Eggleston**：Dye-transfer color, mundane subjects elevated, American South vernacular.
- **Jeff Wall**：Large-scale staged photography, conceptual realism, museum-grade compositions.
- **Alec Soth**：Quiet documentary, loneliness, Americana, road-trip melancholy.
- **Michael Kenna**：Minimalist black-and-white landscapes, long exposure, silence, meditative emptiness.

---

## B. Print & Graphic Engine（平面与插画引擎）

- **Adrian Tomine**：Clean line art, flat muted colors, urban alienation, *New Yorker* sensibility.
- **Hergé**：Ligne Claire, uniform line weight, flat colors, no hatching, clarity and adventure.
- **Hayao Miyazaki**：Ghibli aesthetic, lush landscapes, watercolor textures, flying machines, nostalgia.
- **Roy Lichtenstein**：Pop art, Ben-Day dots, bold outlines, primary colors, comic iconography.
- **Frank Miller**：Chiaroscuro noir, extreme contrast, negative space, splashes of red. *Sin City*.
- **Alex Ross**：Photorealistic gouache, heroic classicism, dramatic lighting, mythic realism.
- **Mike Mignola**：Heavy blacks, gothic folklore, minimalist color, stark compositions. *Hellboy*.
- **Moebius (Jean Giraud)**：Intricate line work, surreal sci-fi fantasy worlds, pastel palettes.
- **Chris Ware**：Complex layouts, isometric diagrams, flat graphic systems, melancholic storytelling.
- **Saul Bass**：Minimalist graphic design, symbolic shapes, bold typography, cinematic motion language.
- **David Aja**：Ultra-clean compositions, flat color blocking, negative space, modern graphic storytelling.

---

## C. 3D & Digital Engine（3D 与数字艺术引擎）

- **Rafael Grassetti**：High-detail character sculpting, realistic skin, mythic realism. *God of War*.
- **Hossein Diba**：Hyper-realistic likenesses, precise facial anatomy, cinematic portrait lighting.
- **Calder Moore**：Stylized sci-fi environments, procedural textures, atmospheric lighting.
- **Zigor Samaniego**：Playful, colorful characters, soft shapes, expressive lettering.
- **Grand Chamaco**：Geometric abstraction, vibrant colors, blind-box toy aesthetics, folk influence.
- **Omar Aqil**：Cubist 3D abstraction, Picasso-inspired forms, metallic and glossy materials.
- **Beeple (Mike Winkelmann)**：Daily dystopian renders, massive scale, pop-culture satire.
- **Ian Spriggs**：Ultra-realistic digital portraits, uncanny realism, detailed skin shading.
- **Pixar Animation Studios**：Soft global illumination, appealing proportions, vibrant colors, subsurface scattering.
- **Blind Box Style (Pop Mart)**：Chibi proportions, matte finish, pastel colors, collectible toy feel.
- **Vitaly Bulgarov**：Hard-surface sci-fi, exoskeletons, industrial cybernetic design.
- **Ash Thorp**：Futuristic UI, cinematic world-building, cyber-industrial aesthetics.

---

## D. Impasto & Fine Art Engine（厚涂与纯艺术引擎）

- **Vincent van Gogh**：Thick impasto, swirling strokes, emotional color intensity.
- **Rembrandt van Rijn**：Chiaroscuro lighting, psychological depth, golden shadows.
- **Claude Monet**：Impressionist brushwork, light vibration, atmospheric color.
- **Jackson Pollock**：Action painting, drip technique, chaotic rhythm.
- **Willem de Kooning**：Aggressive abstraction, distorted figures, tension between form and chaos.
- **Frank Auerbach**：Extreme impasto, sculptural paint, architectural mass.
- **Jenny Saville**：Monumental flesh, visceral texture, distorted perspectives.
- **Lucian Freud**：Granular paint, psychological realism, muted earthy tones.
- **Gerhard Richter**：Blurred realism, abstract squeegee technique, ambiguity between photo and paint.

---

## E. Pixel Art Engine（像素艺术引擎）

- **Waneella**：Moody urban pixel scenes, rain, neon glow, lo-fi atmosphere.
- **Gustavo Viselner**：Cinematic pixel compositions, film-like lighting.
- **eBoy**：Isometric cityscapes, dense detail, playful chaos, Pixorama style.
- **Paul Robertson**：Hyper-kinetic neon pixels, psychedelic retro energy.
- **Matej Jan (Retronator)**：Conceptual pixel art, glitch aesthetics, editorial layouts.
- **Jubilee**：Painterly pixels, soft Ghibli-like tones, nature-centric scenes.
- **Markus “Junkboy” Toivonen**：Blocky retro-modern pixel style, strong palettes.
- **Octavi Navarro**：Narrative-driven pixel art, story-rich environments, cinematic framing.

---

## F. Chinese Ink Engine（中国水墨引擎）

- **Wu Guanzhong**：East–West synthesis, flowing lines, color dots, white walls and black tiles.
- **Qi Baishi**：Expressive simplicity, shrimp and insects, rural vitality.
- **Zhang Daqian**：Splashed-ink landscapes (Po-mo), majestic scale, rich blues and greens.
- **Liu Kuo-Sung**：Modern ink abstraction, textured paper, cosmic landscapes.
- **Xu Bing**：Conceptual ink, experimental calligraphy, linguistic deconstruction.
- **Xu Lei**：Gongbi precision with surreal mood, blue tonal harmony.
- **Fan Zeng**：Classical figures, bold calligraphy, Confucian spirit.
- **Gu Wenda**：Experimental ink, pseudo-script, installation-driven conceptualism.

---

## G. Claymation Engine（定格动画 / 粘土动画引擎）

- **Ray Harryhausen**：Mythic creatures, stop-motion realism, Dynamation.
- **Tim Burton**：Gothic whimsy, elongated forms, macabre fairy-tale tone.
- **Henry Selick**：Dark fantasy, rich textures, meticulous stop-motion craft.
- **Nick Park (Aardman)**：Visible fingerprints, cozy clay textures, British humor.
- **Jan Švankmajer**：Surreal stop-motion, decay, tactile horror.
- **The Brothers Quay**：Dusty miniatures, dream logic, metaphysical unease.
- **Laika Studios**：Hybrid 3D-printed stop-motion, extreme detail, cinematic lighting.
- **Adam Elliot**：Adult claymation, awkward realism, loneliness, bittersweet tone.
